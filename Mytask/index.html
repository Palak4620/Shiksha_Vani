Empowering Blind Students in Remote Education

At Shiksha Vani, we are dedicated to breaking
barriers and ensuring that every student has equal
access to quality education, regardless of their
abilities. In the wake of the pandemic, remote
education has become the new norm, but many blind
students face significant challenges in accessing
educational materials and resources

Approach to Developing AI-Powered Accessibility Solutions for Blind Students in Remote Education

Our approach to addressing the problem of developing AI-powered accessibility solutions for blind
students in remote education involves a comprehensive and collaborative process. We begin by conducting
thorough research to understand the unique challenges faced by blind students in remote learning environments
and to identify existing assistive technologies. This research includes gathering insights from blind students,
educators, and accessibility experts through surveys, interviews, and user testing. Building upon this foundation,
we explore various AI technologies such as natural language processing (NLP), computer vision, and machine learning to
develop innovative solutions tailored to the specific needs of blind learners. Crucially, we prioritize collaboration
with organizations specializing in accessibility and educational institutions to ensure that our solutions align with
curriculum requirements and educational practices.

Through iterative prototyping, user testing, and integration with existing educational platforms, we strive to create
user-friendly and effective AI-powered tools that enhance accessibility for blind students. Additionally, we emphasize
the importance of training, support, and compliance with accessibility standards to ensure the successful implementation
and long-term impact of our solutions.
